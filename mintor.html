Student Loan Solutions

I worked on a solution for student loan debt based on educating people with debt from the point that they start taking out loans into the repayment period. I wanted to educate students on how much their education would cost, give them the resources to calculate their costs while they go to college, then aid with their living expenses and loan repayment once out of college. I designed an app to calculate each of these costs for them.

Research and Decisions

I worked with a partner, focusing on the UX of how the calculator would work, the information architecture of each stage the app would need to assist in, and providing input on the visual design. My goal was for the app to avoid using the language that students typically encounter when signing up for loans. I researched the information that would need to be calculated, and broke it down for each stage. We used blue for the association with financial institutions, with orange for the buttons for contrast and to keep the feeling of the app light. The chevron background was used for texture and visual interest. I later used this research for my work at Freshman Support, working on the financial section.
